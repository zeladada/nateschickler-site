Ithaca Transit

Ithaca Transit is an end-to-end navigation service built for the TCAT bus service in Tompkins County, NY. Users can search for navigation to anywhere in Tompkins County, compare the fastest routes and transfer options, know exactly how late their bus is, see buses on the map in real time, and much more. A free and open-source app, Ithaca Transit offers a diverse range of features in a beautiful, clean interface to help get people where they need to go.
